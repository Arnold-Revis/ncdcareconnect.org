Our Services

At NCD Care Connect, we offer a wide range of services to improve healthcare accessibility for communities affected by non-communicable diseases (NCDs).

Mobile Clinics

We run mobile clinics to reach underserved communities, offering screenings, consultations, and preventive health services. Our team provides education, advice, and resources to help manage and prevent NCDs.

Teleconsultations

Our platform offers online consultations with healthcare professionals, allowing patients to receive expert advice and follow-up care without the need for travel.

Health Education

We provide workshops, webinars, and printed resources to educate the public about preventing and managing non-communicable diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, and more.
