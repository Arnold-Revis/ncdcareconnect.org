Welcome to NCD Care Connect

Transforming healthcare for those living with non-communicable diseases.

Our Mission

We aim to provide accessible, affordable healthcare services and education to prevent and manage non-communicable diseases across Uganda.

Our Story

Founded with the goal of improving healthcare access, we connect patients with doctors, provide education, and run mobile health services.

Our Services

Mobile Clinics: Bringing healthcare directly to communities, providing screenings, consultations, and more.

Consultations & Screenings: Offering teleconsultations, specialized screenings for NCDs, and preventive care.

Health Education: Empowering communities through educational resources and workshops on NCD prevention and management.
